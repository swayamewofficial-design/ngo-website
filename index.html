Vision दृष्टि

Every child from underprivileged families should go to school and receive an education so that they can join the mainstream of both the nation and society.

वंचित परिवारों का हर बच्चा स्कूल जाए और शिक्षित हो, ताकि वे राष्ट्र और समाज दोनों की मुख्यधारा से जुड़ सकें।

Motive उद्देश्य

To accurately assess children's talents, instill dreams in their eyes, and empower them to make those dreams take flight through their own efforts.

बच्चों की प्रतिभा का सही आंकलन कर उनकी आँखों में सपने बोना व स्वयमेव के प्रयासों से सपनों को उड़ान देना।

Mission मिशन

The word "Swayamew" means doing something for oneself, by oneself... that is, that children should be educated, that they should have all-round development, that they should be able to stand on their own feet, and that the country and society should develop.

स्वयमेव का अर्थ स्वयं के लिए स्वयं के द्वारा कुछ करना… अर्थात् बच्चे शिक्षित हों, उनका सर्वांगीण विकास हो, वे अपने पैरों पर खड़े हो सकें, देश और समाज का विकास हो।।

Our Purpose

Why We Do What We Do

At Swayamew we believe that Every child deserves quality education, every woman deserves equal opportunity, and every individual deserves the skills and awareness needed to build a dignified life. Despite significant development, many communities still face barriers such as limited access to education, unemployment, lack of awareness, and gender inequality.

Swayamew was founded with a clear purpose — to create sustainable transformation by empowering individuals to become self-reliant, confident, and capable contributors to society.

Our Work

Projects That Create Impact

Empowering lives through education, skill-building, and creativity—spanning Sunday Pathshala, women empowerment, coaching, and dance initiatives.

Sunday Pathshala

Sunday Pathshala is a weekly initiative by Swayamew where focus is placed not only on children’s education but also on their overall development. Every Sunday begins with prayer and meditation, helping children start learning with a calm and positive mindset. They are provided with academic support and basic learning materials such as stationery and teaching aids. Additionally, activities like T-shirt distribution are organized to give them a sense of belonging. The aim of Sunday Pathshala is to nurture children and guide them towards a better future.

Women Empowerment

At Swayamew – Ek Udaan Sapnon Ki, we are committed to empowering women by creating opportunities for their growth and independence. We have initiated a boutique, Sarojini Boutique, which is successfully run in continuation by one of our dedicated Swayamew coordinators. This initiative helps women learn tailoring, enhance their skills, and become financially self-reliant. Alongside this, we organize medical camps for women and children during our summer programs, focusing on health awareness, hygiene, and basic healthcare access. Through these efforts, Swayamew strives to uplift women socially and economically, enabling them to lead a life of dignity, confidence, and independence.

Coaching Institute

At Swayamew – Ek Udaan Sapnon Ki, we proudly collaborate with Twinkling World Coaching Institute directed by Prashant Sir, located at Kumharpura near Kabeer Ashram, Gwalior. As a trusted partner in promoting higher education, the institute is known for its quality teaching and supportive learning environment. It plays a significant role in strengthening students’ academic foundations and confidence. Many of its students have achieved notable success in their careers, holding respectable positions. Through this partnership, we aim to provide consistent educational support and empower children for a brighter future.

Dance Studio

At Swayamew – Ek Udaan Sapnon Ki, we proudly associate with Nrityashish Dance Studio directed by Ashish Sir, located at Patel Nagar, City Centre. With years of experience, the studio is known for nurturing talent and promoting creativity among students. It has made a meaningful impact on many Swayamew children by encouraging confidence, expression, and discipline through dance. Nrityashish actively contributes by organizing engaging events such as Garba Dandiya Nights and Zumba fitness classes. Through this collaboration, we aim to support holistic development and inspire children to explore their artistic potential.
